Product Requirements Document

Overview

This PRD outlines the key capabilities and requirements for advancing the product platform to better serve our customer base and capture market opportunities in the dynamic commerce space.

Key Requirements

  • Merchandising V1: Support flexible product associations and rule-based bundling
  • Catalog System: Centralized management with rich experiences and maximum flexibility
  • Storefront Experience: Modern, responsive commerce experience across devices
  • Dynamic Pricing: Real-time price optimization based on demand and market conditions
  • Promotions: Powerful discounting and promotional campaign management

Customer Evidence

Validated through 45+ customer interviews, 120+ data points, and comprehensive market research. Key themes include need for advanced catalog flexibility, improved storefront experiences, and dynamic pricing capabilities.

Success Criteria

  • Platform adoption by 60% of customer base within 6 months
  • 15% improvement in average order value for adopting customers
  • Support for 1000+ SKU catalogs with sub-second performance
  • 99.5% system uptime and reliability

Target Release

Q2 2026
